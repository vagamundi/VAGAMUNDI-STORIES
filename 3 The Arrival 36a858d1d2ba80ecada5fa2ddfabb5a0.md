# The Arrival 🚢

![image6.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image6.png)

### There is a great cruise ship at the harbor. 🚢⚓

### It is early morning. 🌅

### The air is fresh. 🌬️

---

### A traveler walks to the reception desk. 🚶‍♂️🛎️

### His coat is elegant. 🧥

### His face is a bit tired. 😮‍💨

![image4.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image4.png)

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Excuse me.”

---

### 👩 Anne: “Hello, Sir. Welcome on board the Vagamundi.” 🚢

### “How can I help you?”

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Hey there… sorry, I’m a bit lost. I have just arrived.”

---

### 👩 Anne: “No worries, Sir. Would you like to check in?” 🗝️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Yes, please.”

---

### 👩 Anne: “Of course. Is there a reservation under your name?” 📖

---

### 👤 Arthur: “I’m not sure, I’m sorry. My name is Arthur Blake.”

---

![image10.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image10.png)

### 👩 Anne: “That’s okay, Mr. Blake… wait a minute, please.” 👀📖

### “Ah, yes! Here it is.”

### “There is a reservation for a quiet room for 3 nights. Is that correct?” 🛏️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Yes, Ma’am.”

![image7.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image7.png)

---

### 👩 Anne: “You can call me Anne. I’m Anne Garcia.” 🙂

---

### 👤 Arthur: “It’s nice to meet you, Anne!”

---

### 👩 Anne: “You too, Sir.”

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Anne, is there a window in this room? I would like to see the horizon.” 🌊

---

### 👩 Anne: “Yes, there is a big window. The view is absolutely beautiful!” 🌊✨

---

![image3.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image3.png)

### 👤 Arthur: “Thank you!”

---

### 👩 Anne: “You’re welcome. Is this your first time sailing with us, Mr. Blake?” 🚢

---

### 👤 Arthur: “You can call me Arthur.” 🙂

### “And yes, it is. Actually, it is my first time sailing ever.” 🌊

### “Everything here is very new to me.”

### “Is the journey safe?” ⚠️

![image8.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image8.png)

---

### 👩 Anne: “The ocean is unpredictable, Sir.” 🌊⚡

### “But this ship is strong, and the crew is excellent.” 👥🚢

### “You are safe here.” 🛡️

![image11.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image11.png)

---

### She opens a wooden box. 📦

### She takes a heavy brass key. 🗝️

![image5.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image5.png)

---

### Arthur smiles. 🙂

### The tension leaves his shoulders. 😮‍💨⬇️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Good. I don’t know the way to the room. Is it far?” 🚪

---

### 👩 Anne: “No, it is not.” 🚶‍♀️

### “There is a long hallway behind you.” 🚶‍♂️

### “Your door is at the end.” 🚪

### “It is room seven.” 7️⃣

![image1.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image1.png)

---

### Arthur takes the key. 🗝️

### His hands are warm now. 🤲

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Thank you for your kindness, Anne.” 🙂

---

### 👩 Anne: “You are very welcome, Captain Blake.” ⚓🙂

### “Enjoy the voyage.” 🚢

![image9.png](3%20The%20Arrival/image9.png)

---

### Arthur looks at the old key in his hand. 🗝️👀

### He turns to the window. 🌊

### He sees the endless sea. 🌊🌊🌊

---

### He is not lost anymore. 🙂

### He is in the right place. 🚢✨

---

### Because a journey is not just about the destination. 🧭

### It is about the people you meet along the way. 👥✨
