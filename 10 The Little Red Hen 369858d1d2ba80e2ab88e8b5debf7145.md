# The Little Red Hen

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/e1a44bba-839c-491e-bf7a-843da0d77fae.png)

### Once upon a time, there was a small farm. It was a simple farm in the countryside. There were green fruit trees, old wooden fences, and small animals. 🚜🍎🌳

### On the farm, there was a large field that was ready to sow. Near that field, there was a hardworking little red hen. 🐔💪

### She thought to herself: “I think we should sow some wheat. I want to make bread!” 🌾🍞

### But she knew it was not easy work. To bake bread, she would have to plant the wheat with care, water the ground, and wait patiently until it was ready. 🌧️⏳

### She thought that the other animals on the farm could help her. She walked past the green trees and looked at them. 👀🐶🐱🐷

### “Who could help me sow this wheat?” she asked. 🤝🌾

---

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/image.png)

### The dog looked at her from the grass. 🐶👀
“Not me,” he said. 🚫
The cat shook her head near the fence. 🐱🙅‍♀️
“I sure can’t,” she said. 🚫
The pig laughed from his warm mud. 🐷😂
“Me neither,” he said. 🚫

### They did not want to do any work. 🚫💪
The hen sighed. “Okay, I need to make a decision.” 🧠
So, the little red hen made time and planted the seeds with care all alone. 🐔🌾

---

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/image%201.png)

### Time passed. The sun was warm, and the rain came. ☀️🌧️
Soon, the empty field changed.

### The wheat grew tall, strong, and golden. 🌾💪✨

### The hen looked at the golden field again. 👀🌾
“The wheat is ready,” she said. “Now, I want to make flour.” 🌾➡️⚪

### “Who can help me take the heavy wheat to the old mill?” 💪
The dog said, “Not me.” 🐶🚫
The cat said, “Not me.” 🐱🚫
The pig said, “Not me.” 🐷🚫

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/image%202.png)

### They just wanted to take a long nap. 💤
So, the little red hen took a deep breath and carried the heavy wheat alone. 🐔🌾💪
Soon, she had clean white flour. ⚪

### The hen looked at the flour. 👀⚪
“Now, I need to make bread.” 🍞
“Who can help me do this job?” 🤝

### The dog said, “Not me.” 🐶🚫
The cat said, “Not me.” 🐱🚫
The pig said, “Not me.” 🐷🚫

### So, the little red hen did the work with care. 🐔💪
She put the ingredients together and mixed the flour, water, and salt. ⚪💧🧂
Then she made the bread and put it in the hot oven. 🍞🔥

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/image%203.png)

### Soon, the warm smell of fresh bread filled the simple farm. 🍞😋
The animals smelled it. They opened their eyes. 👃🍞
The dog came near. 🐶
The cat came near. 🐱
The pig came near. 🐷

### “Can we have some bread?” they asked with a smile. 🍞❓
The little red hen looked at their lazy faces. 🐔👀

![image.png](10%20The%20Little%20Red%20Hen/image%204.png)

### “You did not help me,” she said. 🚫🤝
“I took all the risks, and I did the work.” 🐔💪
“So I will have the reward, and I will get the bread.” 🍞

### She ate the bread alone. 🐔🍞
And everyone had a simple lesson that day on the farm: 🧠
If you didn’t help make it, 🚫🤝
you don’t get to eat it. 🚫🍞
