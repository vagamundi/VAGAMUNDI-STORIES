# Harbor Boutique 🛍️

### There is a small, elegant shop near the harbor. 🏬⚓

### Soft jazz music plays inside. 🎷

---

### Two friends, Ana and Leo, walk into the shop. 🚶‍♀️🚶‍♂️

### A man behind the wooden counter smiles warmly at them. 🙂🪵

---

### 👤 Shopkeeper: “Good morning.”

### 👤 Ana: “Hi there.” 🙂

### 👤 Leo: “Hey… wow, look at this place. Everything looks so nice.” 👀✨

---

### 👤 Shopkeeper: “Welcome to the Harbor Boutique. Is it your first time here?” 🏬

---

![image4.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image4.png)

### 👤 Ana: “Yes, it is.” 🙂

### “We are new in town.” 🌍

---

### Ana walks to a display table. 🚶‍♀️🪑👕

### “Leo, look at this blue shirt.” 👕🔵

### “The color is beautiful.” ✨

---

![image3.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image3.png)

### Leo comes closer. 🚶‍♂️👀👕

### “Hmm… I see it. Nice color.” 👀

### “But look at the size. It looks very big.” 📏⬆️

---

### Ana holds the shirt up. 👕✋

### “You’re right. It is large.” 📏

---

### 👤 Shopkeeper: “Excuse me, can I help you with the sizes?” 🙂

---

![image7.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image7.png)

### 👤 Ana: “Maybe.” 🤔

### “We saw this blue shirt, but it is too large.” 👕❌

### “Do you have a small one?” 📏⬇️

---

### 👤 Shopkeeper: “Ah, yes.” 🙂

### “I see a small one right here.” 👕

### “Here it is. Feel the fabric.” ✋👕

---

![image6.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image6.png)

### Ana touches the shirt. ✋👕

### “Oh, I like it.” 🙂

### “It is very soft.” ✨

---

### Leo laughs softly. 🙂😂

### “Of course you like it. You like every blue shirt in the world.” 👕🔵

---

### Ana looks at the tag. 👀🏷️

### “Wait… Leo, look at the price!” 😮🏷️

### Leo leans closer. 👀

### “How much is it?” ❓

---

### Ana smiles slowly. 🙂🏷️

### “It is… very expensive.” 💸

---

### 👤 Shopkeeper: “It is pure Egyptian cotton, Ma’am.” 🇪🇬✨

### “You can feel the quality.” 👕

---

### Leo walks away to another rack. 🚶‍♂️

### “Hey Ana, look at this over here.” 👀👕

---

### 👤 Ana: “What do you see?” 👀

---

### 👤 Leo: “I see a green jacket.” 🧥🟢

---

![image1.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image1.png)

### Ana walks over. 🚶‍♀️👀🧥

### “Hmm… what do you think, Leo? Is it cool?” 😎

---

### 👤 Leo: “Yes, it is very cool.” 🙂

### “Is it too big for me?” 📏

---

### 👤 Ana: “No.” 🙂

### Leo looks in the mirror. 🪞👀🧥

### “It is perfect.” 👍

---

### Ana smiles. 🙂

### “Look at the price tag, Leo.” 🏷️

---

### Leo sighs. 😮‍💨

### “It is also very expensive.” 💸

---

![image2.png](5%20The%20Harbor%20Boutique/image2.png)

### Ana laughs. 😂

### Leo laughs too. 😂

---

### They didn’t buy anything. 🚫🛍️

### But they are happy. 👀🙂

---

### Sometimes, just looking is a great journey. 👀✨🚶‍♀️🚶‍♂️
