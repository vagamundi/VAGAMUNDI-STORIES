# Stone Soup

![image2.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image2.png)

### Once upon a time, there was a small village. 🏘️

### In the village, the people did not have much food. 🍽️❌

### Their houses were empty, and their pots were cold. 🏠❄️🍲

![image9.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image9.png)

---

### One day, a traveler came to the village. 🚶‍♂️🏘️

### He looked at the people and asked a question. 👀❓

### “Who wants to eat?” 🍲

---

### The people did not say anything. 🤫

### They were tired. 😔

---

### The traveler had a big bag, a metal pot, and a smooth stone. 🎒🍲🪨

### He took the stone from his bag. 🪨

![image5.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image5.png)

---

### Then, he put the pot on the fire. 🍲🔥

### He put water in the pot. 💧

### “I am making stone soup,” he said. 🪨🍲

### “But I need your help to make it great.” 🤝✨

![image3.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image3.png)

---

### A woman came near. 👩🚶‍♀️

### “I have a carrot,” she said. 🥕

### She took the carrot from her pocket. 🤲🥕

### The traveler took the carrot from her hands and put it in the pot. 🥕➡️🍲

---

### “Good,” he said. 👍

### “Let’s make a great meal.” 🍲✨

![image7.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image7.png)

---

### Soon, a man looked at the fire. 👨👀🔥

### “I have a potato,” he said. 🥔

### He took the potato from his bag. 🤲🥔

### The traveler took the potato and put it in the boiling water. 🥔➡️🍲♨️

![image1.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image1.png)

---

### Another person came and said: 👥

### “I have some salt.” 🧂

### The traveler took the salt and said: 🧂➡️🍲

### “Perfect. Now, we have everything.” ✅✨

![image4.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image4.png)

---

### The traveler looked at the full pot. 👀🍲

### He took a spoon and tasted the soup. 🥄🍲

### It smelled fantastic. 👃😋✨

![image6.png](9%20The%20Stone%20Soup/image6.png)

---

### Now, the people did not stay in their empty houses. 🏠❌

### They sat together. 👥

### They ate together. 🍲👥

---

### They saw something simple. 💡

### Alone, they had little. 👤🍽️❌

### But together, they made a great meal. 👥🍲✨

### Many hands make light work. 🤲🤲✨
