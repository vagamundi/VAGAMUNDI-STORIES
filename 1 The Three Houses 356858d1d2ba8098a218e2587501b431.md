## The Three Houses

### Once upon a time, there were three small pigs. 🐷🐷🐷

### Near the pigs, there was a road. 🐷🛣️

### At the end of the road, there was a place to build a house. 🛣️🏠

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%201.png)

---

### The first pig saw the straw. 🐷🌾

### “I see the straw,” he said. 👀🌾

### “I want to make a house.” 🏠

### Soon he made a house of straw. 🏠🌾

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%202.png)

---

### The second pig saw the wood. 🐷🪵

### “I see the wood,” he said. 👀🪵

### “I want to make a house.” 🏠

### Soon he made a house of wood. 🏠🪵

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%203.png)

---

### The third pig looked around. 🐷👀

### He saw bricks. 🧱

### “I see the bricks,” he said. 👀🧱

### “I want to make a strong house.” 🏠💪

### So he took the bricks. 🧱

### He worked all day. ⏳💪

### Soon he made a house of bricks. 🏠🧱

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%204.png)

---

### Then a wolf came down the road. 🐺🛣️

### The wolf saw the straw house. 🐺👀🏠🌾

### “Little pig,” he said, 🐺🗣️

### “let me come in.” 🚪

### “No,” said the pig. 🚫

### So the wolf blew. 🐺💨

### He blew and blew. 💨💨

### Soon the straw house fell. 🏠🌾❌

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%205.png)

---

### The pig ran to the wood house. 🐷🏃‍♂️🏠🪵

### The wolf came again. 🐺

### “Little pigs,” he said, 🐺🗣️

### “let me come in.” 🚪

### “No,” they said. 🚫

### So the wolf blew again. 🐺💨

### He blew and blew. 💨💨

### Soon the wood house fell. 🏠🪵❌

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%206.png)

---

### The pigs ran to the brick house. 🐷🏃‍♂️🏠🧱

### The wolf came again. 🐺

### “Little pigs,” he said, 🐺🗣️

### “let me come in.” 🚪

### “No,” they said. 🚫

### So the wolf blew. 🐺💨

### He blew and blew. 💨💨

![image.png](1%20The%20Three%20Houses/image%207.png)

---

### But the brick house did not fall. 🏠🧱✅

### The wolf looked at the house. 🐺👀🏠🧱

### “It is strong,” he said. 💪

---

### And the pigs learned something simple: 🐷🧠

### What you make with care will stand strong. 🏠💪✨
