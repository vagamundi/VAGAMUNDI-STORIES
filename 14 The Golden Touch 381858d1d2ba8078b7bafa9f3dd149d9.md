### 👑 The Golden Touch

![image.png](14%20The%20Golden%20Touch/image.png)

Long ago, there was a rich king named Midas. He lived in a grand stone palace with beautiful green gardens, but he was completely obsessed with wealth. 🏰✨

King Midas spent his regular days counting golden coins in a dark room. He **wished** he had all the wealth in the world. He **would like** to transform every single object into pure gold. 💰🙅‍♂️

One day, while he sat near his golden treasure, a mysterious messenger appeared from the light. The messenger smiled. *“You have a strong desire, King Midas. Tell me, what do you want? What do you need?”* 🔮

Midas did not hesitate. *“I **want** everything I touch to change into gold!”* The messenger warned him: *“Be careful. Not everything that shines is good for your reality.”* But Midas did not listen. *“That is my ultimate wish!”* he shouted. 🗣️🚫

The next morning, Midas woke up and tested his new power. He touched a simple wooden chair. Suddenly, it changed into heavy, shiny gold. He touched a glass cup. It changed into cold, solid gold.

Midas laughed with pride. *“I have everything now!”* He felt powerful and happy. 👑😂

But soon, his biological reality returned. He walked into the grand dining room because he **needed** real food and water. He took a fresh, soft bread from a silver tray. As soon as he bit into it, the bread changed into hard, metallic gold. He took a cold cup of water, but the liquid turned into solid yellow metal before it touched his mouth. 🍇🥖❌

[Golden Touch.mp4](14%20The%20Golden%20Touch/Golden_Touch.mp4)

Midas grew terrified. Although he had all the gold in the world, he couldn’t eat or drink. *“I don’t want this wealth anymore!”* he cried out in panic. *“I need real water! I need real food!”* 😭🌧️

Suddenly, his young daughter, Aurelia, ran into the room. She saw her father’s sad face and wanted to comfort him. Before Midas could move, she hugged his knees. The moment his hands touched her soft skin, her body went frozen and cold. She changed into a still, golden statue. 👧➡️🗿❌

*“No!”* Midas screamed, his heart breaking into pieces. *“I don't need this curse! I want my daughter back! I need love!”* 💔

![image.png](14%20The%20Golden%20Touch/image%201.png)

He ran past the gardens to find the mysterious messenger. Midas put his hands together and begged: *“Please, can you help me? Can you take this power away?”* 🙏

The messenger looked at his tears. *“If you want your life back, you must give up all your gold. Go to the river, wash your hands, and bring real water to your palace.”* 🌊

Midas took a deep breath. He ran to the river and washed his hands with care. The golden power left his body. He took a jar of normal water back to his home and poured it over his daughter. Aurelia opened her eyes and smiled. She was alive again. 👧❤️🕊️

![image.png](14%20The%20Golden%20Touch/image%202.png)

Midas finally saw the truth. He understood that true wealth is not what you possess. He started to give magnificent gifts to his people and used his energy to help the poor. 👑🤝

 Midas learned a hard lesson that day: when your greed is too big, it is easy **to bite off more than you can chew**. 🎯✨
