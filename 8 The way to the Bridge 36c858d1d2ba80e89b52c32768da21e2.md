# Finding the River 🗺️🌉

![image.png](8%20The%20way%20to%20the%20Bridge/image.png)

### There is a beautiful, historic square in the city. 🌳🏙️

### People walk under the trees. 🚶‍♀️🚶‍♂️🌳

### Cars move slowly. 🚗

---

### Arthur stands near a large map. 👤🗺️

### He looks confused. 😕

### He studies the lines and streets. 👀🗺️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “I don’t know this place.” 🤷‍♂️

---

### A polite woman walks by. 🚶‍♀️🙂

### Arthur raises his hand. ✋

### 👤 Arthur: “Excuse me, Ma’am.”

---

### 👤 Woman: “Yes?” 🙂

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Can you help me?”

### “I’m a bit lost.” 😕

---

### 👤 Woman: “I can try.” 🙂

---

### Arthur points at the map. 👆🗺️

### 👤 Arthur: “I want to go to the river.” 🌊

---

### 👤 Woman: “Ah, the river!” 🙂

### “Okay, it is easy.”

### “Go straight.” ⬆️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Go straight?” 🤔

---

### 👤 Woman: “Yes.” 🙂

### “Go straight down this avenue for two blocks.” ⬆️⬆️

---

### Arthur nods. 👍

### 👤 Arthur: “Two blocks. Got it.” 🧠

---

### 👤 Woman: “Exactly.” 🙂

### “Then, turn left.” ⬅️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Turn left?” 🤔⬅️

---

### 👤 Woman: “Yes.”

### “Do you see that tall white building over there?” 🏢👀

### “Turn left at that corner.” ⬅️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Excellent.” 🙂

### “Do I see the river there?” 🌊👀

---

### Arthur laughs. 😂

### The woman laughs too. 😂

---

### 👤 Woman: “No, not yet.” 🙂

### “After you turn left, walk to the end of the street.” 🚶‍♂️⬆️

### “Go past the old market.” 🏪

---

### 👤 Arthur: “To the end of the street, past the market.” 🧠

### “And what is there?” 🤔

---

### 👤 Woman: “There is a historic stone bridge.” 🌉

---

### Arthur smiles. 🙂

### 👤 Arthur: “So, I see the bridge?” 🌉👀

---

### 👤 Woman: “Yes.” 🙂

### “You see the bridge.” 🌉

### “And the beautiful river is right under the bridge.” 🌊

---

### Arthur reviews the trajectory in his mind. 🧠🗺️

### 👤 Arthur: “So… I go straight for two blocks.” ⬆️⬆️

---

### 👤 Woman: “Yes.” 👍

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Then I turn left at the white building.” ⬅️🏢

---

### 👤 Woman: “Perfect.” 🙂

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Then I walk past the market to the end.” 🚶‍♂️🏪⬆️

---

### 👤 Woman: “Right.” 👍

### “You have a very good internal map, Sir.” 🧠🗺️

---

### Arthur pauses. ⏸️

### 👤 Arthur: “One more question.” ☝️

### “Is it far?” 🤔

### “My legs are a bit tired today.” 🦵😮‍💨

---

### The woman smiles reassuringly. 🙂

### 👤 Woman: “No, it isn’t far at all.”

### “It is a pleasant five-minute walk.” 🚶‍♂️⏱️

---

### 👤 Arthur: “Wonderful.” 🙂

### “Thank you so much for your kindness.” 🤝

---

### 👤 Woman: “You’re very welcome.” 🙂

### “Enjoy your time at the river, Arthur.” 🌊

---

### Arthur begins to walk. 🚶‍♂️

### He follows the trajectory. 🗺️

### He goes straight for two blocks. ⬆️⬆️

### He turns left at the tall building. ⬅️🏢

### He walks past the old market. 🚶‍♂️🏪

### He goes straight to the end of the street. ⬆️

---

### Soon, the great stone bridge appears. 🌉✨

---

### And when you move, the world moves with you. 🚶‍♂️🌍
