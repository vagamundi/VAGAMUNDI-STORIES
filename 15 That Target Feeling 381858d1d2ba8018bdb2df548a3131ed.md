# ***🛒 The Shopping List: Target Hollywood Galaxy***

[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YeKQRaPS6nesrVEZ8)

# ***Outside the Target Hollywood Galaxy, the famous boulevard is a wild world. Many travelers think Hollywood is always clean, nice, and shiny, just like they see it in classic movies. But the reality is completely different. 🎬🍿***

# ***The streets are usually loud and chaotic. People walk past endless souvenir shops. Suddenly, you see breakdancers asking for money right next to actors wearing cheap superhero costumes. The golden stars on the ground are sometimes very dirty. “You must see this place yourself to understand it,” Leo whispered to Anna while they walked through the Hollywood crowd. “It is a very strange experience.” 🦸‍♀️🏙️🚶‍♂️***

# ***They wished to escape the heat of the avenue. Anna looked at her phone. “We are very tired, Leo. We don't need souvenirs, but we need to buy some basic things. We need water and sunblock. There is a Target right here.” 📱☀️🏼***

[https://www.youtube.com/shorts/GI5NHAkKHqM](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/GI5NHAkKHqM)

# ***As soon as they walked into the store, the chaos of the boulevard disappeared. The air was cool, and everything looked clean and organized. But the Target Hollywood Galaxy is not a regular supermarket. It is a palace of desires. 🛒✨***

[We_are_stepping_in_to_target_h.mp4](15%20That%20Target%20Feeling/We_are_stepping_in_to_target_h.mp4)

# ***Anna checked her simple shopping list. “Okay, we must stay focused. We only need two items.”***

# ***Leo nodded, but he looked over there, at the bright displays. “Wow, Anna, look! They have an exhibition of movie cars and life-size action figures here!” 🏎️🤖***

# ***Leo walked past the main corridor. He stood before a beautiful display of electronic products. “Anna, come here. Look at this premium coffee maker. I know our old machine at home works perfectly, but I would like to buy this new one. It makes incredible espresso.” ☕⚙️***

# ***Anna shook her head with a smile. “Leo, you always do this. You want a new coffee maker, but you don't need a new coffee maker. We should save our money for the rest of the trip.” 💸🙅‍♀️***

[https://www.youtube.com/shorts/B54JaX-j1GM](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/B54JaX-j1GM)

# ***"But it looks so cool," Leo muttered. He turned around and noticed another shelf full of limited-edition movie mementos. “Okay, although I can live without the coffee maker, I really want that star wars figure. You can't find this item in Brazil!” 🌌🛸***

# ***Anna sighed, laughing at her friend. “The staff here are usually very helpful, but they cannot save your budget, Leo. If you don't control your desires, you will want to buy the entire store!” 🛍️🛑***

# ***They walked through the red corridors for an hour. In the end, they went to the cashier to pay. Anna looked inside their big cart. They had the water and the sunblock. But they also had a premium coffee maker, two movie t-shirts, a superhero memento, and a box of chocolates. 🍫👕🛒***

# ***Leo smiled and took the heavy bags. “You see? I always come to Target for just one thing, but I never leave with empty hands.” 🛍️🤷‍♂️***

# ***Because in Hollywood, you don't just find actors. You find your own hidden desires. 🎯✨***
