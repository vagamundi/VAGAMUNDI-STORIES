## The Yellow Road

### Once upon a time, there was a famous road that went across a beautiful green land. The road was made of bright yellow stones, and people always said: “Follow the yellow road if you want to find the Emerald City.” 🟨🛣️🌿✨

![image7.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/c16c7b4b-ebaf-45d1-8ccb-185b2b8aac4b.png)

### A young girl named Dorothy was there. She was very far from home because a terrible storm changed her trajectory. She looked at the horizon with sad eyes. 👧💨🏠❌

### “I really want to go home,” she thought to herself. “But I don't know the way.” 💭

### Her small dog, Toto, stood right here next to her, looking confused.

![image9.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/78506424-8d5e-43d6-aaba-e3f5f81ac728.png)

---

### Suddenly, while they walked past a golden field, Dorothy noticed a strange figure on a wooden pole. It was a Scarecrow. 🌾👨 👀

### The Scarecrow looked down at her and spoke with a gentle voice. “Excuse me, traveler. Where are you going in such a hurry?” ❓

![image3.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image3.png)

### “We are going to the Emerald City,” Dorothy explained. “I need to see the Wizard because I want to go back home. What about you? Do you live here?” 🧙‍♂️✨

### The Scarecrow sighed. “Although I can see the field and talk to you, I don't have a brain. I feel empty. I would like to ask the Wizard for some intelligence. Can I join you on your journey?” 🧠💭

### “Of course! Come with us,” Dorothy smiled. “Let's follow the yellow road together.” 🤝🟨🛣️

![image1.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image1.png)

### They walked down the road for hours until they heard a strange metallic sound near the trees. Dorothy stopped and saw a man made entirely of metal. He was the Tin Man, and he stood frozen under the rain. ⚙️👨🌧️

### “Are you okay, Sir?” Dorothy asked with care.

### The Tin Man moved his arms slowly. “I am fine now, thank you. I see you are traveling together. Where are you going?” ❓

![image8.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image8.png)

## “To the great Emerald City,” the Scarecrow answered.

### “We need to see the Wizard.” 🏙️✨

### The Tin Man looked at his metallic chest. “I have a strong body, and I can do a lot of hard work. But I don't have a heart. I cannot feel love or sadness. I want a heart more than anything. I need the Wizard too.” ❤️⚙️

### “Then don't stay here alone,” Dorothy said warmly. “Come with us and follow the yellow road.” 🤝🟨🛣️

---

![image2.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image2.png)

### Now there were three friends moving along the path. But the forest became dark and scary. Suddenly, a huge Lion jumped out from behind the old bushes with a loud roar! 🔊🦁🌲

### Dorothy stepped back, terrified. But instead of attacking them, the big Lion began to cry and hide his face behind his paws. 😨😭

![image10.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image10.png)

---

### “Why are you crying?” *Dorothy* asked, surprised. ❓

“I look big and strong,” the Lion sobbed. “But inside, I am always afraid of everything. I don't have any courage. I would like to be a real king, but I need the Wizard to help me.” 🦁💪❌

### Dorothy smiled and pointed down the avenue of stones. “You are in the right place, my friend. Come with us. We have one road and one goal. We all need the Wizard.” 🎯🤝

---

![image4.png](12%20The%20Yellow%20Road/image4.png)

### Now they were four unique friends walking together.

### Sometimes the road was long and difficult, but they knew they had the strength to continue. 👧🌾⚙️🦁👣🟨🛣️

### Because when your desire is strong, where there is a will, there is a way. 💭➡️
