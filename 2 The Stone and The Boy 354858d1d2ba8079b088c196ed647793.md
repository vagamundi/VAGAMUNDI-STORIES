# The Stone and The Boy

## Once upon a time, there was a stone. 🪨

### It was old, grey, and quiet. 🪨⏳🤫

### The stone was in a field near a castle in old England. 🪨🌾🏰

### There was a sword in the stone. 🗡️🪨

### It was a big, strong sword. 🗡️💪

### “That stone is magic,” people said. 🪨✨🗣️

### There was an old legend. 📜

### “Take the sword,” the people said, 🗣️

### “and you are the King.” 🗡️👑

![image1.png](2%20The%20Stone%20and%20The%20Boy/image1.png)

### Many people are there for it. 👥

### They are big and strong. 💪

### They see the sword. 👀🗡️

### They try to take it. ✋🗡️

### But the sword is still in the stone. 🗡️🪨❌

### Arthur is there too. 👦

### “He’s a young boy,” said a woman. 👩🗣️

### “He’s just a helper,” said a man. 👨🗣️

### He is not a knight. 🚫🛡️

### He is not a king. 🚫👑

### Arthur sees the sword. 👀🗡️

### “There is a sword,” he says. 🗣️

### “It is in the stone.” 🪨

### Merlin is there. 🧙‍♂️

### The old man sees Arthur. 👀👦

### “Arthur,” he says, 🗣️

### “You are ready.” ✅

![image6.png](2%20The%20Stone%20and%20The%20Boy/image6.png)

### Arthur shakes his head. 🙅‍♂️

### “I am only a helper,” he says. 🤲

### “I am not a king.” 🚫👑

### Merlin smiles. 🙂

### “Take the sword,” he says. 🗣️🗡️

### Arthur walks to the stone. 🚶‍♂️🪨

### His hand is on the sword. ✋🗡️

![image3.png](2%20The%20Stone%20and%20The%20Boy/image3.png)

### He takes the sword. ✊🗡️

### The sword moves. ⚡

### Now the sword is free. 🗡️🕊️

### Now the sword is in Arthur’s hand. ✊🗡️

### The people see the boy. 👀👦

### They see the sword. 👀🗡️

### They say, 🗣️

### “You are the King.” 👑

![image8.png](2%20The%20Stone%20and%20The%20Boy/image8.png)

### Arthur looks at the sword. 👀🗡️

### “I am Arthur,” he says. 🗣️

### Merlin nods. 👍

### “Yes,” he says. 🗣️

### “And now everyone can see it.” 👀✨

### Arthur was not a king before. 👦🚫👑

### But now it is clear. 👀🔍

### The boy and the king are the same. 👦🟰👑

![image5.png](2%20The%20Stone%20and%20The%20Boy/image5.png)

### And sometimes, the truth is simple. ✨

### The truth is written in stone. 🪨📜
