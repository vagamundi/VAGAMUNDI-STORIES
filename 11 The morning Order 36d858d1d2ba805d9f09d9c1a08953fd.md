![{00D599EA-4B9A-491C-8844-4E88703F3B91}.png](11%20The%20morning%20Order/00D599EA-4B9A-491C-8844-4E88703F3B91.png)

New York City regular days are very busy, loud, and fast. But inside this café on Fifth Avenue, the world is calm and inviting.

![{AC69C12F-7CC8-4EC1-B0C6-315D6F32B575}.png](11%20The%20morning%20Order/AC69C12F-7CC8-4EC1-B0C6-315D6F32B575.png)

Leo and Anna sit in a quiet corner. They look at a map, but their faces are sad. They have an important situation to discuss.

![{C83D8CF8-786A-49EF-A4F4-2A2C10C53E82}.png](11%20The%20morning%20Order/C83D8CF8-786A-49EF-A4F4-2A2C10C53E82.png)

Leo shakes his head. *"I don't know this street, Anna. This map is not clear."*

A waitress walks over to their table with a warm, professional smile. *"Good morning. Welcome to WatchHouse. How can I help you today?"*

Anna looks up from the paper. *"Excuse me. Yes, please. We are a bit lost. Can you help us?"*

*"Of course,"* the waitress says, looking at their map. *"Where do you need to go?"*

Leo points at a line on the paper. *"We need to find the old harbor office, but we don't see the right street."*

The waitress looks at the street outside the window and then points down the avenue. *"Ah, I know the place. It is easy. Look over there. You need to follow this avenue."*

*"Go straight?"* Leo asks.

*"Yes, Sir. Go straight for two blocks. Right here on this corner, you turn left. Walk past the central bank. Down the road, there's a big pharmacy on the right. The harbor office is right next to it."*

Arthur looks at the lines again. *"So, we go straight, turn left at the corner, and past the bank. Then down the road there's a pharmacy."*

*"Perfect,"* the waitress nods. *"The office is right there."*

Leo smiles, and the tension leaves his shoulders. *"I see. Thank you so much for your help."*

*"You're very welcome,"* she says. *"Now, are you ready to order breakfast?"*

*"Yes, we are,"* Anna says. *"I'll take a hot tea and a chocolate croissant, please."*

*"And I'll take a large black coffee and the butter toast,"* Leo says.

*"Excellent,"* the waitress says. *"I'll get them for you at the counter right now."*

Soon, she comes back with a wooden tray and puts the cups on the table. The coffee is rich, and the bread is perfect.

![{7550EA78-D12F-4751-8434-ADA0CE472FA7}.png](11%20The%20morning%20Order/7550EA78-D12F-4751-8434-ADA0CE472FA7.png)

Leo takes a sip and looks at his friend. *"This place is great. Her directions are very clear."*

Anna looks at the view outside. *"Yes. You see? We are in the right place."*

Because a journey is not just about the destination. It is about the people you meet along the way.
